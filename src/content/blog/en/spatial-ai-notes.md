Chat works well for linear tasks, but struggles to represent changing relationships among many objects. Spatial interfaces offer another way to encounter intelligent systems.

## Relations before answers

In space, a system can reveal how material relates before offering a conclusion. People can enter through structure instead of accepting a single answer.

## The camera is interaction

Focusing, pulling back, and changing viewpoint are not merely visual effects. They determine which information enters attention. An intelligent interface should treat its camera as an explainable recommendation mechanism.
