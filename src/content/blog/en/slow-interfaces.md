We often treat response speed as a synonym for interface quality. Yet in creative work, research, and consequential decisions, an immediate response does not always imply understanding.

## Delay and rhythm

Delay is a system failing to respond. Rhythm is a system responding at the right time. The difference depends on whether intention is visible and whether the person can steer the process.

## Designing a pause

A pause can establish hierarchy and create room to reconsider an irreversible action. It should have a legible reason, rather than using animation to waste time.

Real speed reduces the cognitive backtracking needed to reach a goal. It is more than shaving milliseconds from every click.
